Visualization of Our Timeline (Fall 2019 - Spring 2020)

Figure 1: Gantt Chart for Team Lora.

This Gantt chart (see Figure. 1) displays the schedule of our capstone project. We have three phases for next semester, Library Development, Back End Development, Test App Development / Finalizing. The red line near the top represents where the team is currently in the timeline. Each task blow is represented by a colored box that shows the duration of how long we should be working on each task. December 18th to February 19th we will be working on library development. From February 1st to March 18th we will be working on the back end development. The remainder of the semester we will be testing our project and finalizing the project. Though this chart is obviously subject to change, especially on exact due dates, the overall progression should remain constant.
